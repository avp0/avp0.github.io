About

I am a mechanical engineering student at UC Berkeley focused on aerospace, defense, and hardware systems. I like problems where mechanical design, analysis, manufacturing, and mission context all have to fit together.

My work spans eVTOL development, precision mechanical systems, robotics, product development, and policy-adjacent engineering research. I have led student aircraft design work, contributed to hardware development in a national laboratory environment, and built prototypes that move from CAD to physical testing.

I am strongest in the early-to-mid stage engineering loop: defining requirements, building concepts, analyzing loads and interfaces, fabricating prototypes, and iterating toward hardware that is useful in the real world.
