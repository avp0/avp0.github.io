About

Built early. Still building.

I am a mechanical engineering student at UC Berkeley focused on aerospace, defense, and hardware systems. I like problems where mechanical design, analysis, manufacturing, and mission fit together.

My work spans eVTOL development, precision mechanical systems, product development, and policy-adjacent engineering research. I have led student aircraft design work, contributed to hardware development in a national laboratory environment, and built prototypes that move from CAD to physical testing.

I am strongest in the early-to-mid stage engineering loop: defining requirements, building concepts, analyzing loads and interfaces, fabricating prototypes, and iterating toward hardware that is useful in the real world.

If you want to talk hardware, aerospace, defense technology, or mechanical design, feel free to reach out on LinkedIn.
