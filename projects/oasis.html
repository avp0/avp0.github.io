Background

Travelers and students often face unsafe navigation conditions due to poorly lit streets, lack of monitoring, or limited access to real-time safety data. Existing navigation apps prioritize speed over security, leaving a gap for solutions that address personal safety. To address this, our team at the European Innovation Academy conceptualized Oasis ‚Äî a navigation app that prioritizes safety by guiding users through well-lit, CCTV-monitored routes while integrating real-time incident reporting and community-driven updates.

Goals

The project aimed to:
  • Design a mobile app that provides the safest route options using active, real-world safety data
  • Incorporate a real-time reporting system verified by multiple users to reduce misinformation
  • Develop a profitable business model, partnering with local businesses, universities, and more
  • Partner with local governments, universities, and law enforcement to improve route data reliability
