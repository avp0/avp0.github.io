Background

Many travelers and students often encounter unsafe conditions due to poorly lit streets, lack of monitoring, or limited access to real-time safety data. Existing navigation apps prioritize speed over security, leaving a gap for safety-oriented routing . To address this, our team at the European Innovation Academy developed Oasis ‚Äî a navigation app that prioritizes well-lit, CCTV-monitored routes and enables community-driven, real-time incident reporting to improve personal safety during navigation.

Goals

The project aimed to:
  • Build a mobile app that prioritizes the safest routes using active, real-world safety data
  • Incorporate real-time incident reporting with multi-user verification to reduce false alerts
  • Develop a profitable business model combining freemium tiers, B2B partnerships, and in-app ads
  • Collaborate with local governments, universities, and law enforcement to enhance data accuracy
