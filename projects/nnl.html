Bettis Atomic Laboratory Internship

*Work done under DOE L Clearance

  • Relevant Skills: Creo, GD&T & ASME B/Y14.5 Standards, Structural Analysis (MathCAD), Product Development, DFMA, Rapid Prototyping/3D Printing, COTS Component Integration, Mechanical Testing and Analysis, Technical Documentation, Procurement

Background

During Summer 2025, I interned at Bettis Atomic Laboratory, where I supported mechanical design work for specialized spent-fuel examination equipment. The project involved precision hardware operating in a high-consequence environment, so the design had to balance alignment accuracy, structural margin, maintainability, and integration with existing equipment. Due to national security considerations, only high-level details can be shared.

Goals

I led the end-to-end design and prototyping of a high-value payload leveling mechanism with tight angular accuracy requirements. The work required ASME Y14.5-compliant documentation, COTS hardware selection, load-case analysis, vendor coordination, and cross-disciplinary integration with adjacent mechanical and operational systems.

Design and Execution

  • Developed multiple mechanism concepts in Creo, using datum schemes, tolerance stackups, and ASME drawing practices to control critical interfaces
  • Performed MathCAD structural checks across representative load cases to evaluate stresses, deflection, and safety factors
  • Selected COTS components and specialty alloys based on load capacity, dimensional precision, availability, and compatibility with the assembly environment
  • Used additive manufacturing for fit checks, packaging studies, and fast iteration before committing to higher-cost hardware
  • Coordinated with cross-functional engineering teams to resolve interface constraints and verify fit within the larger equipment envelope

Outcome

The resulting prototype achieved ±0.1° leveling accuracy and improved the reliability of the handling workflow. The experience strengthened my ability to move from requirements to CAD, analysis, procurement, prototype build, and design review in a regulated engineering environment.
