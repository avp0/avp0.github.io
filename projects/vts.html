Vaccinate The States

  • Relevant Skills: Data Aggregation, Volunteer Training, SQL Databases, Web Scraping, API Development, Team Leadership, Public Health Communication

Situation

During the COVID-19 pandemic, vaccination site data was fragmented, outdated, or inaccessible. Major pharmacies like Safeway and CVS lacked centralized information on vaccine types, availability, and eligibility. To address this gap, I joined Vaccinate The States, a volunteer-led nonprofit that aimed to build and maintain the largest nationwide map of vaccination sites, empowering millions to access vaccines quickly and safely.

Task

Our goals were to:
  • Aggregate and update real-time vaccine site data across all 50 states.
  • Train and organize a large-scale volunteer base for phone and web banking efforts.
  • Transition from local California efforts to a nationwide system with county-level data.
  • Ensure partnerships with public health agencies to make vaccine data accessible and transparent.

Action

  • Data Collection:
    • Started as a phone banker, calling hundreds of vaccine providers and logging availability into SQL databases.
    • Promoted to phone banking captain, training 350+ volunteers in data entry, site calls, and reporting.
    • Transitioned to web banking, developing scrapers with the engineering team and collating data for 3,000+ U.S. counties.
  • Technology & Automation:
    • Streamlined data analysis by integrating the Discord API to reduce repetitive manual tasks.
    • Helped implement a county-level collation system for structured site tracking nationwide.
  • Leadership & Collaboration:
    • Led volunteer teams as both a phone and web banking captain, balancing training, data validation, and communication.
    • Interfaced with Marin County Health Department, convincing them to restore daily vaccine site updates to improve accessibility for non-technical residents.
  • Scaling Nationwide:
    • Expanded operations from 1 state to all 50 states, mapping 75,000+ vaccine sites in real time.
    • Maintained one of the most comprehensive nationwide COVID-19 vaccine maps, widely recognized during the pandemic.

Result

  • Built and maintained the largest vaccine site database in the U.S., covering 75,000+ locations.
  • Trained 350+ volunteers, creating efficient workflows for national operations.
  • Directly influenced Marin County’s reporting practices, contributing to their having one of the highest vaccination rates in the nation.
  • Enabled millions of Americans to find accurate vaccination information during a critical public health crisis.

Conclusion

Vaccinate The States demonstrated the power of grassroots, tech-enabled public health initiatives. From training hundreds of volunteers to expanding from California to a 50-state operation, this project showed how collective action and data-driven solutions can save lives. Personally, it taught me team leadership, large-scale data management, and the importance of equitable access to information — lessons I carry forward into every project I tackle.
