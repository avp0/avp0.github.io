Project Engagement

  • Ongoing Project: DoW–NASA Logistics Interoperability
    Currently participating in the inaugral cohort of the Berkeley Security and Resilience Accelerator (Hacking for Defense), an intensive, project-based national security course. My team's ongoing project, sponsored by SpaceWERX, applies the Lean LaunchPad methodology to examine how the Department of War’s ten-class supply taxonomy and NASA’s six functional logistics categories compare in terms of function, criticality, governance, and life-cycle intent. Our team is conducting stakeholder discovery with defense, intelligence, and space-sector partners, analyzing alignment and mismatches between the two systems, and developing relevant recommendations toward a shared framework to improve interoperability in joint or shared missions.

Project scope and other components will likely change as a result of ongoing stakeholder discovery and project evolution.
