Project Engagement

  • Ongoing Project: DoW–NASA Logistics Interoperability
    Participating in the Berkeley Security and Resilience Accelerator (Hacking for Defense), an intensive, team- and project-based national security course. Sponsored by SpaceWERX, this ongoing project applies the Lean LaunchPad methodology to examine how the Department of War’s ten-class supply taxonomy and NASA’s six functional logistics categories compare in terms of function, criticality, governance, and life-cycle intent. The team (Ad Astra) is conducting stakeholder discovery with defense, intelligence, and space-sector partners, analyzing alignment and mismatches between the two systems, and developing policy-relevant recommendations toward a shared framework or common language to improve interoperability in joint or shared missions.
